Project Aegis Mechanical Design Specification - Battery Module

1. Component: Battery Holder Assembly (Part # MECH-001)
   - Overview: 4S2P Battery holder configuration.
   - Compatible Cell Type: 18650 Cylindrical Cells ONLY.
   - Slot Diameter: 18.2 mm (Max tolerance +0.1mm).
   - Slot Length: 65.2 mm (Spring loaded).
   - Material: ABS Plastic (UL94-V0).

2. Thermal Interface
   - Passive cooling via aluminum chassis contact.
   - Max design thermal load: 20W per module.

3. Constraints
   - Total module height cannot exceed 70mm due to enclosure limits.
   - Weight budget: 500g per module.
